About JustTheJob

A portal for both employers and prospective employees which aims to automate the job hunting and hiring process in the most efficient and accurate way possible. It is one of a kind among all the job portals. An intelligent chat bot would interact with the user and help him/ her build their resume.

What sets us apart?
The entire hiring process is automated, there is no room for bias and we also provide a One of a kind reccomendation system which ensures that a prospective employee adds additional skills to his/her candidature so as to i improve resume and hence increase chance of selection.
